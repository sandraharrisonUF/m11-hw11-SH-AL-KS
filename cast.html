Cady Heron

Cady Jane Heron is a main character and the protagonist in Mean Girls. She is portrayed by Lindsay Lohan. Cady is a student at North Shore High School who moved to Evanston after living her last 12 years in Africa.

Regina George

Regina George is the main antagonist of the movie Mean Girls and leader of The Plastics. Regina is portrayed by Rachel McAdams. She is intelligent, manipulative, and capable of doing anything to get what she wants.

Gretchen Wieners

Gretchen Wieners is one of the main characters in Mean Girls. She is portrayed by Lacey Chabert. Gretchen is a member of the Plastics. She is the gossip girl in the group and the loyal sidekick of Regina George.

Karen Smith

Karen Smith is one of the main characters in Mean Girls. She is portrayed by Amanda Seyfried. Karen is a member of the Plastics, and she's shown to be extremely ditzy and completely clueless on many occasions, and she is easily influenced.
